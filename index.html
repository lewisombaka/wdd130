Nairobi

Nairobi, the capital city of Kenya, is a bustling metropolis known for its unique juxtaposition of urban development and natural beauty. From the towering skyscrapers in the central business district to the wildlife roaming freely in Nairobi National Park, the city offers a diverse range of experiences. Its vibrant markets, rich cultural heritage, and thriving arts scene make it a dynamic destination for visitors and a vibrant home for its residents.
